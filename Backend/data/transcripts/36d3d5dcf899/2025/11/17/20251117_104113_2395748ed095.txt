We live in an era of unprecedented information overload. The internet provides immediate access to a wealth of knowledge, news, and opinions. While this offers undeniable benefits, it also brings significant challenges. The sheer volume of content, ranging from factual reports to misleading propaganda and "fake news," necessitates an essential tool: critical thinking. The ability to objectively analyze and evaluate information is no longer merely an academic skill, but a necessity for informed decision-making and a well-functioning society.
