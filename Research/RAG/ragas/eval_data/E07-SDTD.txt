Saturday, March 8th. Slow morning, coffee and reading until 10. Met Daan and Sofie for brunch in the city — the place on the Vestdijk, eggs and sourdough. We were there for almost three hours just talking. I forgot how much I miss that, sitting somewhere with no agenda. Afternoon I went for a long walk alone, probably 8km, listened to music. Felt really good in my body for the first time in a while. Evening was quiet. Made a grocery list for the week, which sounds small but it's the kind of thing I let slip when I'm not managing myself well. Slept well. Want more Saturdays like this.
